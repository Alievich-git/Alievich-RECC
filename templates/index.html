Step 5: Look at the left menu: Click App Settings -> Basic. Your App ID will be right there.
Step 6: ⚠️ You must Publish the app! In the top navbar, toggle the "App Mode" switch from Development to Live. (Note: You may be required to paste a Privacy Policy URL in Basic Settings before it switches).

Step 3: Click Add. Name the user and give them an Admin role.
Step 4: Click Add Assets. Select your Ad Account and Facebook Page, giving the system user "Full Control" over them.
Step 5: Click Generate New Token. Select your new Meta App from the dropdown list.
Step 6: Crucial: Check these 4 boxes: ads_management, pages_manage_ads, pages_read_engagement, and leads_retrieval.
